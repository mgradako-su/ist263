Megan's Biography

Personal Info

My name is Megan Radakovich. I am a dual Graphic Design & Information Management and Technology major, interested in typography and web design.

Some of my accomplishments include creating three original typefaces and designing the logo and literature for a local political candidate in my hometown of Cincinnati, Ohio. I am skilled at using the Adobe Suite, and I consider myself very organized and responsible.

Outside of work, I love:

  • doing arts and crafts
  • watching The West Wing or New Girl
  • hanging out with the people I love

I also spend too much time scrolling through Pinterest, and I love going to garage sales to see what I can find.
